Unreal Engine

Unreal Engine is a free* to use software to create almost anything you can image: from virtual worlds to the visually stunning games. He has not just one but two (or maybe even three) videos 5 HOURS each! And you know what even more insane about it, it is absolutely free. But please do not afraid this amount of time. It is more than just worth it.
